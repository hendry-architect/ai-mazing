# 06 — Trust

The family and succession layer: continuity of ownership, incapacity
protocols, and the sealed instruments the Stewardship Council depends on.

**Belongs here**

- Family trust governance documents (governance side — legal trust instruments
  live with counsel and are referenced, not stored, here)
- The sealed Stewardship Council initial roster (PEGS-101 §3)
- The incapacity determination process (PEGS-101 §2, trigger 2)
- Family employment and stewardship expectations (with `10-Policies`),
  anchored to earned-not-inherited (PEGS-000 Art. III §7, Art. VI §6)

**Rules of this folder**

- Highest-sensitivity folder in PEGS: access restricted to the Founder and
  named counsel until succession documents say otherwise.
- Nothing here overrides PEGS-000 — the trust serves the Constitution, not
  the reverse.

**Status:** Reserved — populated as succession instruments are drafted.
**Custodian:** Founder.
