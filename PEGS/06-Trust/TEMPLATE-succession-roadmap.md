# Founder Succession Roadmap

> The calm, written path for the highest-stakes transition. Connects
> PEGS-101 (who holds constitutional authority) with the human and legal
> steps that make it real. Sensitive — access per `06-Trust` rules.

## Horizons

| Horizon | State | Founder's role | Milestones |
|---|---|---|---|
| Now → +2y | Founder-led | All Class 1/2 | Stewardship roster named (sealed); deputies real for every executive role |
| +2y → +5y | Delegation deepens | Class 1 + few Class 2 | Operators run entities; Founder chairs, teaches, writes |
| +5y → +10y | Steward-ready | Constitutional only | Council activated by written delegation for defined domains (PEGS-101 §2.1) |
| Contingency (any day) | Incapacity/death | — | PEGS-101 activation; instruments below execute |

## Instruments checklist (legal — with counsel; status tracked here)

- [ ] Will & revocable trust aligned with PEGS ownership map
- [ ] Powers of attorney (financial, healthcare) naming per PEGS-101 logic
- [ ] Incapacity determination process signed (feeds PEGS-101 §2.2)
- [ ] Sealed Stewardship Council roster current (reviewed annually)
- [ ] Buy-sell / transfer provisions for each entity
- [ ] Key-person insurance in force (register: risk-compliance library)

## The people work (the part paper can't do)

Successor development per heir-education guide · operators told their
role in each scenario BEFORE the scenario · family walked through the plan
annually at a family meeting — no surprises at the worst moment.

## Annual review

Every July with the constitutional review: roster, instruments, readiness
— one page of deltas filed here.
