# Family Meeting — <YYYY-MM-DD>

> Twice yearly minimum (one may fold into the annual retreat's closed
> session). Runs on the Meeting Kit, family edition: same discipline,
> warmer table. Records file to `06-Trust/family-records/` — never to the
> general meeting records.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Present | <adult members; next-gen for designated segments> |
| Facilitator / Notes | <rotates — not always the Founder> |

## Standing agenda

1. **The table** (no business) — how is each person, actually. 20 min
   minimum; this is the meeting's real infrastructure.
2. **State of the Enterprise for the family** — plain-language update:
   what's growing, what's hard, what it means for us (no PHI, no
   employee-personnel details).
3. **Wealth & stewardship** — one topic per meeting from the stewardship
   policy (spending rhythm, giving, education funds, a legal instrument
   explained).
4. **Next generation** — heir-path updates, in the heir's own voice.
5. **Decisions & asks** (DECISIONS / ACTION ITEMS tables per Meeting Kit —
   family decisions recorded like any others).
6. **Gratitude round** — close as a family, not a board.

## Rules

- Disagreements spoken at the table, never triangulated afterward
  (family constitution §5).
- Enterprise decisions stay in Enterprise governance — the family meeting
  informs and advises; it does not instruct executives.
- What is said at the table stays at the table.
