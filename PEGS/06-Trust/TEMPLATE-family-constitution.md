# Pascual Family Constitution — <draft vX>

> The family's own charter: how the family relates to the Enterprise and to
> each other across generations. Subordinate to PEGS-000 where the
> Enterprise is concerned; sovereign in family matters. Drafted WITH the
> family, not for it — this template structures that conversation.

## 1. Who we are

Family purpose statement · the story we tell about where we came from ·
what "Pascual" should mean in 100 years.

## 2. Values (inherit from PEGS-000 Art. III, add family-specific)

The seven Enterprise values, plus the family's own — e.g., presence at the
table, service before status, both languages spoken at home.

## 3. Family & the Enterprise

- **Employment:** family members earn roles by competence and character —
  identical process, identical reviews (PEGS-000 Art. III §7, Art. VI §6).
- **Ownership vs. management:** owning does not mean running; the family
  raises stewards and hires the best operators, family or not.
- **Voice:** how family concerns reach governance (family meeting → Founder
  / council), never via hallway veto.

## 4. Wealth philosophy

What wealth is FOR (see TEMPLATE-wealth-stewardship-policy.md): sustaining
people and mission across generations — not status, not idleness.

## 5. Decision-making & conflict

Family meeting cadence (TEMPLATE-family-meeting.md) · how disagreements are
handled (spoken directly, never triangulated) · a named neutral elder/
advisor for deadlocks.

## 6. Education of the next generation

Commitment to the heir-education path (GUIDE-heir-education.md) as a
condition of stewardship roles.

## 7. Amendment

By family council per its own rule (e.g., 2/3 of adult members), recorded
in writing; never in conflict with PEGS-000 for Enterprise matters.

**Signatures — each adult member, with date.**
