> the daily operating system of the Pascual Enterprise. Every meeting
> produces standardized outputs (Meeting Kit), updates the knowledge base,

> workstreams remain separate, integrated later through qualified

## Deployment rules

1. **The calendar is law:** events run on their scheduled week even if
   imperfectly prepared — a rough rhythm beats a perfect intention
   (Systems Over Heroics).
2. **Every meeting runs the Kit.** No agenda → not convened; no minutes →
   didn't happen; no follow-up in 48h → escalation to the chair.
3. **The knowledge loop is mandatory:** minutes → records → quarter index
   → annual index → next plan's §1. Durable lessons → `14-Knowledge-Base`
   ratified learnings. A meeting that feeds nothing forward was a
   conversation, not governance.
4. **Founder-mode is temporary by design:** each cadence element names
   its future non-Founder chair; delegation happens as roles instantiate
   (03-Executive-Leadership) — the rhythm is built to be handed off.
5. **Legal stays separate:** nothing in this plan forms, transfers, or
   papers anything; counsel's workstreams integrate on their own track.
