# The Quarterly Review Process (week 12 → week 13, every quarter)

> Composes three ratified templates into one repeatable close:
> quarterly report → retreat → next-quarter lock. Runs Sep/Dec*/Mar/Jun.
> (*Q4 close is absorbed by the Strategic Enterprise Summit.)

## The sequence

| Step | When | Action | Artifact |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1. Data freeze ⚙ | Week 12, Mon | Dashboards pulled: KPI actuals, OKR evidence, financials, tracker closure stats, register movements | Data pack |
| 2. Quarterly report drafted | Week 12 | Owner drafts from TEMPLATE-quarterly-report — grades honest, misses in the same font as hits | Q report v0.9 |
| 3. Entity inputs | Week 12 | Each entity lead contributes their section (Pascual Inc., Wellnex, AllMed per shareholder rhythm) | Entity sections |
| 4. Retreat packet | Week 13, T-7d | Q report + draft next-quarter plan + risk register + prior retreat's action status | Packet |
| 5. **Quarterly retreat** | Week 13 | Per TEMPLATE-quarterly-retreat: truth → one big conversation → plan lock → team health → commitments | Retreat report ≤72h |
| 6. Plan lock | At retreat | Next-quarter plan (OKRs, initiatives, risk review, capacity honesty) signed | Locked plan |
| 7. Archive close ⚙ | Week 13, Fri | `records/YYYY/QN/` closes with the quarter index (meetings, D-IDs, closure rate) | Quarter index |
| 8. Knowledge feed | Week 13, Fri | **PEGS-950 entry filed (the four questions — LL-YYYY-NNN)**; durable "should-become-standard" items → canon PR; KPI register updated; profile TO CONFIRM deltas applied | LL entry + KB updates |

## Quality bar

Every OKR graded with evidence · every KPI floor breach explained with
action taken · report readable by a family member · retreat report filed
within 72h · zero unarchived meetings in the closing quarter.

## Roles

Founder chairs (→ delegates as roles instantiate) · Secretary function
runs steps 1, 4, 7 · every entity lead owns their section — a missing
section is escalated, never ghost-written.
