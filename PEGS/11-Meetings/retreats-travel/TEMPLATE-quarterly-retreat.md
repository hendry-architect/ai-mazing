# Quarterly Retreat — <YYYY-QN> — <location>

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Date / duration | <YYYY-MM-DD, half/full day> |
| Attendees | Leadership team |
| Facilitator / Notes | <rotate — the Founder should get to think, not only chair> |
| Packet | T-7d: quarter scoreboard, OKR grades, action-tracker closure stats |

## Agenda (the arc: truth → meaning → commitment)

| Block | Time | Content |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Quarter in truth | 90m | OKR grades KR-by-KR; KPI floors; what we said vs. did (from the record, not memory) |
| 2. One big conversation | 90m | A single strategic tension chosen by the Founder in the packet — not a status parade |
| 3. Next-quarter plan lock | 90m | Draft quarterly plan pressure-tested and locked (planning-system template) |
| 4. Team health | 45m | Capacity honesty check; what we stop doing; recognition |
| 5. Commitments round | 30m | Each leader states their quarter commitment aloud — the room remembers |

## Report (file within 72h — this section IS the retreat report)

- Quarter grade + two sentences of honest narrative
- DECISIONS table (Meeting Kit format, D-IDs)
- ACTION ITEMS table (owners, due dates) → tracker
- The plan as locked (link)
- What we're stopping (as important as what we're starting)
