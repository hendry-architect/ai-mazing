## The decision

Commit the Enterprise to Mission-001: the First Annual Ascendencia
Summit™ at the Hotel Hermitage Monte-Carlo, Dec 27 2026 – Jan 2 2027,

## Why now, and what happens if we do nothing

The Summit is the capstone of the annual planning cycle and the
birthplace of the 2027 Enterprise Strategy. Doing nothing: NYE
availability evaporates, the planning cycle loses its Movement 3
(pressure-test), and the tradition's first edition — the hardest one to
found — slips a year.
