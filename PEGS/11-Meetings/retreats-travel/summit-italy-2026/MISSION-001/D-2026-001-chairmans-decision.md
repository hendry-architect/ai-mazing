# CHAIRMAN'S DECISION D-2026-001
## Subject: Approval of Mission-001 – Ascendencia Summit™ 2026

> Filed verbatim by the CGO. This is the first decision issued under the
> Chairman's record-identifier convention (D-YYYY-NNN) and rules on
> [DECISION-MEMO-M001](DECISION-MEMO-M001.md).

---

After review of Mission-001, its strategic objectives, operational
readiness assessment, financial planning assumptions, governance
framework, and risk analysis, I determine that Mission-001 aligns with
the long-term vision of the Pascual Enterprise.

**Mission-001 is hereby approved to proceed to Operational Phase B.**

This approval authorizes the commencement of planning activities,
including but not limited to:

- Reservation of accommodations, subject to cancellation terms
  acceptable to the Enterprise.
- Evaluation and procurement of airfare at the appropriate purchase
  window.
- Budget development and tracking.
- Meeting agenda refinement.
- Strategic preparation activities.
- Governance testing through PEGS.
- Creation of all operational records required under PEGS.

This approval does not authorize expenditures outside the approved
Mission budget without additional approval where required by the
Authority Matrix.

Mission success shall be evaluated not only by the quality of the Summit
itself but by the effectiveness of PEGS as the Enterprise Operating
System.

**Approved.**

**Chairman**
**Dr. Hendry Julian Perez Pascual**
