# MISSION-001 — Ascendencia Summit 2026
## The Enterprise's first live operational mission

> **Mission Charter.** Mission-001 executes the First Annual Ascendencia
> Summit™ (Hotel Hermitage Monte-Carlo, Dec 27 2026 – Jan 2 2027) as a
> live, end-to-end test of PEGS. Every decision, approval, expense, and
> action flows through EXISTING PEGS artifacts — no parallel processes.
> The mission validates that PEGS functions as a real enterprise
> operating system under live conditions; its planning phase completes
> the validation BEFORE the August 3 cadence launch.
