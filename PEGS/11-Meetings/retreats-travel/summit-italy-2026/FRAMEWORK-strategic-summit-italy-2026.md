# First Annual Strategic Enterprise Summit
## Hotel Hermitage Monte-Carlo · December 27, 2026 → January 2, 2027

> **The annual institution, edition one.** Seven days on the Riviera —
> Monte-Carlo, Monaco, with Italy thirty minutes east for the cultural
> blocks — designed to feel like the founding of a tradition, not a
> vacation with meetings. Primary purpose: **design the next decade of
> the Pascual Enterprise.** By summit week, PEGS will have run live for
> five months; the summit is the culmination of a governed year, not the
> system's debut.

## The seven days (Founder's structure)

| Day | Date | Theme | Sessions | Output |
|---|---|---|---|---|

| **2** | Sun Dec 28 | **Enterprise Review — the five pillars** | Morning: Healthcare (Pascual Inc. + AllMed) + Education (Wellnex) · Afternoon: IP (Holora + marks), Real Estate (Los Gonsos + premises question), Philanthropy (Foundation status, IHC relationship) — Q4/annual close accepted | Year-in-truth page · Q4 report accepted |

| **4** | Tue Dec 30 | **Innovation · AI · Long-term Initiatives** + culture | Morning **Innovation Session**: AI roadmap, automation wave, Wellnex platform, Sana Diferente™ · Afternoon **cultural block**: Italian Riviera excursion (Ventimiglia/Sanremo) or Monaco heritage — deliberately unproductive | Innovation priorities · rested minds |
| **5** | Wed Dec 31 | **Family Legacy · Stewardship · Succession · Values** | **Stewardship Council Session** (pre-activation format if roster named; family legacy conversation regardless) · succession roadmap year-review · heir-path notes · **Reflection session** before New Year's Eve dinner | Closed-session notes → 06-Trust · NYE as family, on purpose |
| **6** | Thu Jan 1 | **Build & Approve the 2027 Strategic Plan** | Plan v0.5 → pressure-test against the decade position → objectives, KPI commitments, initiatives, NOT-doing list, budgets → **2027 Enterprise Goals fixed** | Plan v0.9 + **Annual Declaration signed** (first act of the year) |

Daily rhythm: mornings open with 30 unscheduled minutes · sessions run
the Meeting Kit (decisions + actions captured live) · one evening fully
free beyond Day 4 · the **daily 10-line log** feeds the report.

## The Annual Declaration (new instrument, signed Jan 1)

One page, signed at the summit each year: the year completed in three
sentences · the decade line · the 2027 goals (3–5) · the one thing the
Enterprise will NOT do · signatures of all present. Filed in canon;
becomes the tradition's spine — read aloud at the next summit's Day 1.
