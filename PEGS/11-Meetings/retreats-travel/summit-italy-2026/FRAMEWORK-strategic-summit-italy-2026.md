# Strategic Enterprise Summit — Italy · December 2026

> The Enterprise's first international strategy summit (travel playbook,
> international protocol) — and the centerpiece of the first annual
> planning cycle. One trip serves four governance functions: Q4 close ·
> annual retreat · 2027 plan pressure-test · family/legacy session.
> **Class 2 decision:** the summit proceeds on a completed decision memo
> (budget threshold + international) — memo prepared at T-60.

## Parameters

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Dates | Dec 14–18, 2026 (Founder-adjustable; 5 days incl. travel) |
| Location | Italy — city/venue selected at T-60 (criteria: quiet, walkable, workroom with natural light, ≤90 min from intl. airport) |
| Participants | Founder + invited ELT members + family per closed-session design; advisors if seated by then |
| Coverage | Full authority-continuity note before wheels-up (playbook rule): who holds what, in writing |
| Budget | TEMPLATE-trip-budget-itinerary instantiated at T-60; contingency 10% |
| Devices & data | PHI-clean devices; VPN; no firewalled work over hotel networks (playbook §security) |

## The decade question (the summit's reason to exist)

Named by the Founder in the T-60 frame memo. Candidate framing for 2026:
*"What must be true by 2036 for the Enterprise to be governed, not just
owned — and what does 2027 have to start?"* One question, held all week.

## The arc (five days)

| Day | Block | Output |
|---|---|---|
| 1 — Arrive | Travel · evening: the year told as a story (no slides — the annual index read aloud in parts) | Presence |
| 2 — Truth | Q4/annual close: KPI review findings · four quarterly reports · what we said vs. did · maturity gate honesty (150.009) | Year-in-truth page + Q4 report accepted |
| 3 — The question | Morning deep work on the decade question · afternoon inputs (readings, advisor calls, walks) · evening synthesis | Position paper draft |
| 4 — The plan | 2027 annual plan v0.5 pressure-tested against the decade position: objectives, KPI commitments, initiatives, NOT-doing list · entity budgets reviewed | Plan v0.9 + budget conclusions |
| 5 — Legacy + depart | **Closed family/legacy session** (records → 06-Trust only): succession roadmap year-review, heir-path notes, family voice on the decade question · commitments round · travel | Closed-session notes · commitments |

Protected time daily (playbook rule): mornings start with 30 unscheduled
minutes; one evening fully free — the best synthesis historically arrives
at dinner, not on slides.

## Outputs (the standardized chain)

Summit report ≤72h · 2027 plan v0.9 → ratification by Jan 15 · decade
position paper → knowledge base · closed-session notes → 06-Trust ·

Movement 4 of the planning cycle — the summit is not a trip with
meetings; it is governance that happens to travel.
