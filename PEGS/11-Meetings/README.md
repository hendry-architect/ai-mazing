# 11 — Meetings

The rhythm layer: recurring meeting charters, agendas, and the written record
of decisions — because a decision without a written record did not happen
(PEGS-000 Art. VI §1).

**Belongs here**

- Meeting cadence canon: which recurring meetings exist, who attends, what
  each is for — and which meetings deliberately do NOT exist
- Standing agendas per meeting type
- Minutes and decision records, filed `YYYY/YYYY-MM-DD-<meeting>.md`
- The annual constitutional review record (PEGS-000 Art. VI §7)

**Rules of this folder**

- Class 2 decisions made in any meeting get a decision memo from
  [12-Templates/DECISION-MEMO-TEMPLATE.md](../12-Templates/DECISION-MEMO-TEMPLATE.md);
  minutes alone are not sufficient for one-way doors.
- Dissents are recorded with the decision, never edited out (Art. V §3).

**Status:** Active — cadence canon drafted in Phase 4.
