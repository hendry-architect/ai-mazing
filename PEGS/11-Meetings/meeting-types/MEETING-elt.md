# Meeting Type — Executive Leadership Team (ELT)

> The Enterprise's operating room. Two forms, one membership, everything
> on the Meeting Kit. This card defines the ELT; the agenda mechanics
> come from the ratified planning-system templates — never duplicated
> here.

## Form 1 — ELT Weekly (50 min)

Runs `planning-system/TEMPLATE-weekly-leadership-meeting.md` exactly
(metric minute · action review ⚙ · priorities · blockers & handoffs ·
one decision · firewall & flag check), with two ELT-standing rules:

- **Patient-safety preemption:** any patient-safety item raised by any
  member moves to the top of the agenda immediately — the firewall
  outranks the format.
- **Entity round:** the metric minute covers Pascual Inc., Wellnex, and
  AllMed each week — no entity goes dark for a week.

## Form 2 — ELT Monthly (Monthly Executive Review, 90 min)

Runs `planning-system/TEMPLATE-monthly-executive-review.md` exactly
(scoreboard ⚙ · by-exception · reallocations · risk & compliance pulse ·
wins), with the ELT addition:

- **§6 People pulse (5 min):** hiring, capacity, and morale flags per
  entity — the org chart vs. the SOP index check (design principle 10).

## Preparation checklist (chair + secretary, every session)

- [ ] T-7d weekly / T-48h monthly: agenda issued from template, §1
      pre-filled from tracker ⚙
- [ ] Dashboard current (no live data hunting in the room)
- [ ] Decision items teed with class identified (Class 2 → memo attached)
- [ ] Absent members' §1/§3 lines collected in writing beforehand
- [ ] Prior follow-up report's corrections window closed

## Outputs (the standardized chain — no exceptions)

Kit minutes (DECISIONS + ACTION ITEMS) → follow-up ≤48h → tracker +
decision log updated → filed `records/YYYY/QN/` → feeds the monthly
scoreboard and the quarterly report. Durable lessons → knowledge base by
PR.
