# Enterprise Planning System

One cadence stack, four altitudes. Each level answers a different question
and feeds the one below; all meetings run on the Meeting Kit.

| Level | Question | Rhythm | Artifact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Annual** | What must be true 12 months from now? | Set at the annual retreat, ratified by Jan 15 | [TEMPLATE-annual-plan.md](TEMPLATE-annual-plan.md) |
| **Quarterly** | Which 3–5 outcomes this quarter? (OKRs + initiatives) | 2nd week of each quarter | [TEMPLATE-quarterly-plan.md](TEMPLATE-quarterly-plan.md) |
| **Monthly** | Are we on trajectory? What must change? | 1st Monday | [TEMPLATE-monthly-executive-review.md](TEMPLATE-monthly-executive-review.md) |
| **Weekly** | What moves this week? What's blocked? | Fixed weekly slot | [TEMPLATE-weekly-leadership-meeting.md](TEMPLATE-weekly-leadership-meeting.md) |

**Writing goals well:** [GUIDE-okr-kpi.md](GUIDE-okr-kpi.md) — OKRs (the
change we're making) vs. KPIs (the health we're guarding), plus the KPI
register.

**Risk integration:** every quarterly plan reviews the risk register
(`10-Policies/risk-compliance/TEMPLATE-risk-register.md`) — planning that
ignores its risks is optimism, not strategy.

**Rules**

- Plans cascade: a quarterly objective that serves no annual objective is
  cut or the annual plan is amended — never silently both.
- Actuals come from the dashboard (packet §2), not from memory.
- The stack is the Founder's leverage: once running, weekly and monthly
  levels are chaired by executives, not the Founder (delegate outcomes).

**Automation hooks:** cadence pre-calendared for the year ⚙ (governance
calendar); OKR/KPI tables sync to dashboards (Sheets/Notion) per
INTEGRATION-STANDARD; overdue plan artifacts auto-flag in the weekly agenda.
