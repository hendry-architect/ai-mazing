# Weekly Leadership — <YYYY-MM-DD>

> 50 minutes, same slot every week, Meeting Kit applies (lightweight mode:
> agenda + minutes tables; packet is the standing dashboard link).

| Segment | Time | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Metric minute | 5m | Each leader: one number that mattered this week |
| 2. Action review | 5m | Tracker items due/overdue — status only, no stories ⚙ |
| 3. Priorities | 10m | Each leader: top priority this week + confidence /10 |
| 4. Blockers & handoffs | 15m | "I need X from Y by Friday" — named, dated |
| 5. One decision | 10m | Max one Class 3 decision teed in advance; Class 2 → memo + monthly/ad-hoc |
| 6. Firewall & flag check | 5m | Anything approaching a firewall, compliance date, or brand risk — say it now |

## Standing rules

- Starts on time regardless of who is present; missing leaders send their
  §1/§3 lines in writing beforehand.
- Problems surfaced here are never punished; problems hidden here always
  are (PEGS-000 Art. IV §7).
- Anything needing >10 minutes becomes a scheduled working session with the
  right people, not a hijacked weekly.
