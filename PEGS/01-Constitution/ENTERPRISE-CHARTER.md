# The Pascual Enterprise — Charter

> One page, carried everywhere. **PEGS-000 is the authoritative text**; this
> charter is its pocket edition for onboarding, packets, and daily use.

**Mission** — The Enterprise exists to sustain human beings — patients,
readers, listeners, teams, and generations — through systems of care,
knowledge, and wellbeing that outlive any single person. *No curo. Sostengo.*

**Vision** — A multi-generational enterprise rooted in medicine, known for
trust (the most trusted bilingual voice in health), craft (nothing ships we
would not sign), and endurance (still governed by these principles in 100
years).

**Core values** — 1 Sostengo: we sustain · 2 Human dignity first ·
3 Integrity before advantage · 4 Excellence as the standard · 5 Systems over
heroics · 6 Dos idiomas, una voz · 7 Stewardship, not ownership.
*(Each value forbids real behavior — PEGS-000 Art. III.)*

**Leadership principles** — Clarity is kindness · Think in decades, act in
weeks · Evidence → conviction → action · Slow at one-way doors, fast through
two-way doors · Delegate outcomes, not tasks · Automate the repeatable,
humanize the exceptional · Surface problems early · Protect the firewalls ·
Never trade the brand for the quarter · Challenge ideas hard, respect people
always. *(PEGS-000 Art. IV.)*

**Decision doctrine** — Class 1 constitutional (Founder/Council) · Class 2
one-way doors (written memo + Five Gates + Founder) · Class 3 reversible
(the accountable role: act, record, inform). Five Gates: Mission ·
Integrity · Standard · Leverage · Legacy — one failed gate is a veto.
*(PEGS-000 Art. V.)*

**Governance philosophy** — Written canon, one authoritative location ·
explicit ratification, never drift · subsidiarity with accountability ·
three permanent firewalls (clinical/legal/PHI, brand, compliance floor) ·
automation serves the mission · stewardship and succession by design.
*(PEGS-000 Art. VI.)*

**Supremacy** — Everything references PEGS-000. Nothing gets built before
it. Nothing gets built against it.
