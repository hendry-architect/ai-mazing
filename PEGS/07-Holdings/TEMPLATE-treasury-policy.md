# Treasury & Cash Management Policy — <Enterprise / HoldCo>

## 1. Accounts & structure

One operating account per entity (no commingling — ever) · consolidated
visibility dashboard ⚙ (balances auto-pulled weekly) · signatories exactly
per the Authority Matrix — bank records reconciled to the matrix quarterly.

## 2. Controls

| Control | Rule |
|---|---|
| Dual control | All new payees; any transfer > $<X> |
| Payment authority | Per Authority Matrix tiers; no verbal payment orders |
| Reconciliation | Monthly, by someone who cannot initiate payments |
| Wire discipline | Callback verification on new/changed instructions (top fraud vector — no exceptions, including "the Founder emailed me") |

## 3. Liquidity ladder

| Tier | Amount | Where | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Operating | <N> weeks of expenses | Checking | Payroll & payables, always |
| Reserve | Per capital policy §1.2 | High-yield/treasuries | Shock absorber |
| Strategic | Opportunity fund | Per investment policy | Deployed only via Class 2 |

## 4. Intercompany cash

Documented sweeps/loans only (see intercompany guide) · month-end
intercompany balances reconciled and papered · no entity ever pays another
entity's obligations directly.

## 5. Reporting

Weekly cash position to the leadership meeting §1 ⚙ · 13-week rolling
forecast monthly · days-cash KPI on every dashboard with its floor alarmed.
