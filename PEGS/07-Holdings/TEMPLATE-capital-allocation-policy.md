# Capital Allocation Policy — <Enterprise / HoldCo>

> Written before the first big check forces the question. Every dollar of
> free cash flow has a rank-ordered destination; exceptions are written
> decisions, not moods.

## 1. Order of operations (free cash flow waterfall)

1. **Compliance & safety floor** — never underfunded: licensure, insurance,
   clinical standards, security.
2. **Reserves** — until each entity holds <N> months of operating expenses
   and the Enterprise holds <N> months consolidated.
3. **Reinvestment in compounding systems** — automation, people, brand
   assets that pass the Leverage Gate.
4. **Opportunity fund** — acquisitions/new ventures per PEGS-100 §4–§5;
   capped at <X>% of liquid assets.
5. **Distributions** — per wealth-stewardship policy; after 1–4, never
   instead of them.

## 2. Hurdles

| Use | Minimum bar |
|---|---|
| Internal project > $<X> | Written one-pager; expected return or capability named; Five Gates |
| Acquisition | Class 2 memo; PEGS-000 adoption as closing condition; integration owner named before LOI |
| New entity | PEGS-100 §4 lifecycle complete |
| Debt | Serves a named asset; stress-tested at +<X>% rates; never against control assets |

## 3. Review

Quarterly at the monthly review nearest quarter-end: waterfall actuals vs.
policy · annual re-ratification with the annual plan · every deviation has
a decision record.
