# 07 — Holdings

The capital layer: the future holding structure that owns the operating
companies, and the policies that govern the Enterprise's money.

**Belongs here**

- Holding company charter and ownership map (which entity owns what)
- Capital allocation policy: how profits are reinvested, reserved, or
  distributed — written before the first big check forces the question
- Acquisition criteria and pipeline records (PEGS-100 §5: PEGS-000 adoption
  is a closing condition of every acquisition)
- Risk register: the named risks the Enterprise carries and who watches each

**Rules of this folder**

- Every capital decision above the threshold set in the capital allocation
  policy is Class 2 (written memo, Five Gates).
- Legal formation and tax documents live with counsel; this folder holds the
  governance logic that instructs them.

**Status:** Reserved — chartered when the holding structure is formed.
**Custodian:** Founder.
