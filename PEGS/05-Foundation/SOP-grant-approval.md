# SOP — Grant Approval Workflow

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Owner | Foundation lead (Founder until formed) |
| Trigger | Grant proposal received or program grant cycle opens |

## Thresholds

| Grant size | Approver | Requirements |
|---|---|---|
| ≤ $<small> | Program owner | Criteria check + log entry |
| ≤ $<mid> | Foundation lead | Full review below |
| > $<mid> or multi-year | Board (or Founder pre-board) | Full review + meeting decision (D-ID) |

## Procedure

1. **Intake ⚙** — proposal into the grant log (one row: org, ask, program
   fit, contact, date).
2. **Eligibility screen** — mission fit (giving pillars), 501(c)(3) or
   fiscal sponsor verified, no insider benefit (self-dealing check —
   automatic decline + note if tripped).
3. **Review** — capability (can they do it?), outcomes (what changes, for
   whom, measured how?), sustainability (what happens when our money
   stops?). One-page written recommendation.
4. **Decision** — per thresholds; recorded (log + D-ID where board-level).
   Declines get a respectful written reply within 14 days — how we say no
   is part of the brand.
5. **Award ⚙** — grant letter (amount, period, reporting dates); reporting
   dates → compliance calendar automatically.
6. **Follow-through** — grantee report at period end; results into the
   impact dashboard; renewal only after report review.

## Quality bar

No grant without a written outcome expectation; no renewal without a
report; log current within 7 days of any event.
