# 05 — Foundation

The philanthropic arm: how the Enterprise gives, teaches, and serves beyond
commerce — the mission (PEGS-000 Art. I) expressed as giving.

**Belongs here**

- Foundation charter: mission, giving pillars (expected: bilingual health
  education, community wellness, physician mentorship), funding policy
- Grant-making criteria and annual giving record
- Governance-side documents only — legal formation documents live with counsel
  and are referenced, not stored, here

**Rules of this folder**

- Forming the Foundation is a Class 2 decision (written memo, Five Gates,
  Founder ratification per PEGS-000 Art. V).
- The Foundation inherits PEGS-000 on day one, like every entity (PEGS-100 §4).
