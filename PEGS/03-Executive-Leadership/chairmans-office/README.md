# The Chairman's Office

> The Founder's permanent governance cockpit — one place where everything
> that needs the Chairman's attention, decision, or memory lives. Operated
> by the Chief Governance Officer function (currently: the AI CGO under
> the Founder's authority, per the AI usage policy — every output human-
> reviewed, nothing here decides; it prepares decisions).

## Operating rules of the office

1. **The office prepares; the Founder decides.** Nothing here holds
   authority — it concentrates attention (PEGS-150.006 unchanged).
2. **One-way ingestion:** the office PULLS from canon sources (decision
   log, tracker, registers, calendar) and never forks them — projections,
   not copies (design principle 7).
3. **Sensitivity:** Founder Notes and personal sections are the Founder's
   private space in the repo; anything Trust-grade goes to `06-Trust`,
   never here; nothing from this office syncs externally without the
   knowledge-sync SOP's rules.
4. **The 15-minute contract:** the weekly brief must be readable in 15
   minutes, the monthly book in 45. If it takes longer, the CGO cut it
   badly — compression is the office's craft.
5. **Succession-ready by design:** every instrument here is what a future
   chief of staff inherits on day one — the office is the CoS role,
   documented before it is hired (design principle 8).

## Custodianship

Custodian: Founder. Operator: CGO function. The office's own health is a
scorecard row — an unrefreshed dashboard is a red light, visibly.
