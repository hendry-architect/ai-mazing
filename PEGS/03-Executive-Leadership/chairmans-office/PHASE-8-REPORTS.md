## 10. Recommendations — the first 90 days (Aug 3 → Oct 31)

1. **Delegate two chairs by Oct 1:** COO chairs ELT weekly by September;
   a named secretary function (hire, assign, or formalize CGO-automation
   interim) by October — the single highest-leverage move against risk #1.
2. **Place the Hermitage hold this month** (by Aug 15) — the only
   deadline in this phase that the market enforces, not us.
3. **Instantiate the three registers in September** (KPI, risk,
   compliance-with-dates) — the difference between a governed quarter
   and a documented guess.
4. **Pass the first four PEGS-000 adoption resolutions** (Pascual Inc.,
   Wellnex, Holora, Atemporal — resolution template's first live test)
   at September's monthly review — entities formally adopt the canon
   they already follow.
5. **Protect the rhythm ruthlessly in August** — hold four ELT weeklies
   even if imperfect; the system's reputation is set in its first month.
6. **Don't build anything new before November** — no new documents, no
   new automations beyond the CGO-manual pair; run what exists and let
   the October KPI review tell us what's actually missing.
