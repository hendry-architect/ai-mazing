# Governance Operations Center — the engine room

> How the rhythm actually runs: the four cadence dashboards, the
> recurring workflows behind them, the trigger/reminder schedule, and
> the follow-up checklists. Everything maps to an Automation Catalog row
> — CGO-manual until its automation ships, identical output either way.

## 1. The four dashboards (views, not new data)

| Dashboard | Composition | Refreshed |
|---|---|---|
| **WEEKLY** | Tracker pulse (due/overdue/new) · this week's calendar · decision queue deltas · one flag | Fridays, with the Weekly Brief (AUT-014) |
| **MONTHLY** | KPI scoreboard vs floors · D-IDs of the month · compliance 30-day lookahead · scorecard deltas | 1st Monday, feeds review §1 + the Book (AUT-013) |
| **QUARTERLY** | OKR grades · closure rates · register movements · maturity gate progress · archive status | Quarter close, feeds the report (AUT-008) |
| **ANNUAL** | Annual index · scorecard year-view · KPI re-charter results · maturity declaration evidence | July, feeds the constitutional review |

## 4. Follow-up checklists (what "done" means, per event type)

**After any meeting:** minutes filed ≤24h □ · follow-up sent ≤48h □ ·
tracker rows created with dates □ · D-IDs logged □ · Class 2 memos
attached □ · records path correct □.
**After any quarter:** report issued □ · retreat report ≤72h □ · plan
locked □ · archive index closed □ · scorecard refreshed □ · lessons PR'd □.
**After any trip:** report ≤72h □ · spend reconciled □ · actions in
tracker □ · contacts filed □ · devices checked □.
**After any ratification:** statuses flipped □ · registry updated □ ·
sync run (W-7) □ · decision log row □.

## 5. Escalation to the Chairman

The Ops Center escalates only: a red scorecard metric · a missed
compliance date (always) · a firewall touch (always, immediately) · a
decision-queue item past its need-by · a workflow that failed twice.
Everything else is handled at the cadence — that's what it's for.
