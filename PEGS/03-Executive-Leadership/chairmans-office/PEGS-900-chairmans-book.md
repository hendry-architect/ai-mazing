# PEGS-900 — The Chairman's Book

> The Founder's private monthly briefing book: everything a chairman
> needs, compiled — never authored — from canon sources. Editions are
> numbered **PEGS-900.YYYY-MM**, filed in `chairmans-office/book/`,
> readable in 45 minutes. Compilation is AUT-013 (CGO-manual until the
> automation ships). **Private instrument:** syncs only to the Founder's
> own systems; never to shared projections.

## Compilation rules

1. Compile, don't create — every line traces to a source; new analysis
   belongs in §0 only, marked as the CGO's synthesis.
2. Deltas over states — what CHANGED this month leads every section.
3. Honest emptiness — a section with nothing new says "no change," never
   filler.
4. The summary earns its page — if §0 can't say what matters in one page,
   the month wasn't understood.
5. Editions are immutable once issued; corrections appear in the next
   edition's errata line.
