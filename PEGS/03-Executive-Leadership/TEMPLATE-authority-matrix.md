|---|---|---|---|---|

## Instrument-specific rules

| Instrument | Minimum tier | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Employment offers (non-executive) | B | Executives: A only |
| Contracts / vendor agreements | per tier $ limits | Legal review above $<X> |
| Bank / treasury movements | A (B with dual control ≤ $<X>) | Dual control always for new payees |
| Clinical protocols, PHI access grants | Licensed authority only | Firewall — no financial tier applies |
| Public statements / press | A (brand firewall) | Company social per ratified brand canon |
| Real estate, debt, equity, IP transfer | A | Always Class 2 memo first |

## Maintenance

Reviewed annually with performance reviews; every change is a PR (who/what/
why in the commit). Emergency deviations: act to prevent harm, document
within 24h, review at next leadership meeting.
