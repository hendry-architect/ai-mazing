# SOP — Executive Onboarding & Offboarding

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Owner | Founder (until a people role is chartered) |
| Trigger | Signed executive offer / accepted resignation / termination |

## Onboarding (first 30 days)

**Week 0 (before day 1)**
- [ ] Role charter finalized and signed (TEMPLATE-role-charter.md)
- [ ] Authority Matrix updated by PR; signature limits live on day 1
- [ ] Accounts and access provisioned to the role's tier — nothing broader ⚙
- [ ] Reading path issued: PEGS-000 → Enterprise Charter → their role
      charter → Authority Matrix → their entity's canon (order matters)

**Week 1**
- [ ] Constitution conversation with the Founder (60 min): values as
      constraints, firewalls, decision classes — not a slideshow, a dialogue
- [ ] Meets every direct report and each peer executive 1:1
- [ ] Joins the weekly leadership meeting (observer voice, full candor)

**Weeks 2–4**
- [ ] Drafts their 90-day plan against the mandate; reviewed by the Founder
- [ ] Signs confidentiality + conflict-of-interest acknowledgment
- [ ] First decisions made in-class, with the memo habit demonstrated

**Day 30 check:** can they state the mission, their decision rights, and
their Class 2 triggers unprompted? If not, the onboarding failed — fix it,
don't blame them.

## Offboarding (any exit, any reason)

- [ ] Access revoked at the agreed hour; treasury/banking dual-control
      re-keyed same day ⚙
- [ ] Authority Matrix + role charter updated by PR (interim per succession
      plan takes effect immediately)
- [ ] Knowledge handover per succession-plan checklist; owned SOPs
      reassigned in `09-SOP`
- [ ] Open action items reassigned in the action tracker ⚙
- [ ] Exit conversation: what should the Enterprise learn? Filed to
      `14-Knowledge-Base` (ratified learnings)
- [ ] Departure communicated inside 48h — dignity always, regardless of
      circumstances (Art. III §2)

## Escalation

Any exit involving a firewall breach or legal exposure → Founder + counsel
before any other step.
