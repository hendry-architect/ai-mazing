# 03 — Executive Leadership

Roles and decision rights: the layer that moves decisions off the Founder's
desk without moving accountability off the map.

**Belongs here** (Phase 3 deliverables)

- The delegation matrix: every recurring decision classified (Class 2/3 per
  PEGS-000 Art. V §1) and assigned to a role
- Role charters: one per executive seat — mandate, decision rights, escalation
  triggers, deputy path
- Escalation canon: the Class 2 triggers each role must recognize

**Rules of this folder**

- Every role charter names exactly one accountable holder and one deputy path
  (PEGS-000 Art. VI §6).
- A decision right not written here does not exist — it remains with the
  Founder by default.

**Status:** Reserved — unblocks when Phase 2 is ratified.
**Custodian:** Founder until the first role charters are ratified.
