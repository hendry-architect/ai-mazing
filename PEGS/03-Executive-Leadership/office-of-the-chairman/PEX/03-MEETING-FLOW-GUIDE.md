# PEX™ 3 — The Meeting Flow Guide
## "What happens before, during, and after every meeting"

Every meeting in the enterprise follows one flow. You only appear in the
middle of it — everything on either side is done for you.

## Before (you do nothing)

Three days ahead, the agenda is prepared from what's actually open — the
action tracker, the decision queue, the calendar. Anything needing a
decision arrives as a short pre-read. You get one packet, ready. If a
packet hasn't reached you 48 hours before a monthly review, that is the
system's failure — say so.

## During (this is your part)

You chair. The agenda is timed; you keep it moving. Two phrases matter:

- **"Decision:"** — say it before anything you want ruled and recorded.
  It gets a D-number and enters the permanent log.
- **"Action:"** — say it before anything you want done. It gets an owner
  and a date in the tracker. No owner and date = it doesn't exist.

Everything else is conversation, and conversation is fine — it just
isn't a record.

## After (you do nothing, again)

Within **24 hours**: minutes are filed, with a DECISIONS table and an
ACTIONS table — never prose summaries of who-said-what.
Within **48 hours**: every action owner gets their follow-up with the
date attached.
Then the tracker and decision log are updated, and the meeting is done.

If Friday's brief doesn't confirm this chain completed, the meeting
isn't finished — no matter how good it felt.

## The meetings you'll actually sit in

| Meeting | Rhythm | Length | Your role |
|---|---|---|---|
| ELT (you, Dr. Barroso Perez, Martha) | Weekly, Monday | 50 min | Chair |
| Monthly Review | First Monday | 90 min | Chair |
| Quarter Close + Retreat | Last week of quarter | Half day | Chair |
| Ascendencia Summit™ | Dec 27 – Jan 2 | 7 days | Chair |
| Stewardship / Advisory Council | When formed — quarterly | 2 h | Chair |

Each has its own ID series (ELT-2026-001, and so on). You never assign
IDs — Claude does.

## Asking about any meeting

Plain words work: *"What did we decide at the last ELT?"* · *"What
actions are still open from June?"* · *"Prepare Monday's agenda."*
