# PEX™ 8 — The Executive Cheat Sheet
*One page. Everything.*

**Your job:** Lead. Decide. Sign. Everything else is prepared for you.

## The week
**Mon** — chair ELT, 50 min (first Monday: Monthly Review, 90 min) ·
**Fri 3 pm** — read the Weekly Brief · **Fri 4 pm** — private
reflection, 3 questions · **Anytime** — rule on memos, 5 min each.

## The year
Quarter closes → Oct–Nov KPI review → **Dec 27–Jan 2 Ascendencia
Summit™** → **Jan 1 sign the five Declarations** → July constitutional
review.

## Deciding
Memo arrives → read 5 min → four questions (mission? sign my name?
our standard? lasts?) → rule in a sentence + **Confidence: High/Med/Low**
→ Claude logs it with a D-number.
**Class 1** = constitutional, 30-day reflection. **Class 2** = one-way
doors, memo required. **Class 3** = your team's, not yours.

## In meetings, two words
**"Decision:"** → it gets ruled and logged. **"Action:"** → it gets an
owner and a date. Everything else is just conversation.

## Only you
One-way doors · your name and the SOSTENGO brand · constitutional
changes · clinical/legal/privacy (the firewall — never delegated).

## Never you
GitHub · formatting · filing · minutes · remembering deadlines —
deadlines come *to* you.

## Say to Claude
"Show me my dashboard" · "What needs my decision?" · "Prepare a memo
for…" · "Approved — confidence high" · "File the minutes" · "Where does
M-001 stand?" · "File a lesson: …" · **"What needs me?"**

## If something feels wrong
Say it early, out loud. Surfaced problems are rewarded; hidden ones are
the only offense.

*No curo. Sostengo.*
