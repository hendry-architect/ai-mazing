# PEX™ 6 — The Dashboard Guide
## "How to read your one screen"

The **Chairman's Dashboard** is the single screen of everything awaiting
you. It's refreshed every Friday. You never edit it (except two sections
that are yours alone) — you read it, and you ask Claude about it.

To see it, just say: **"Show me my dashboard."**

## The sections, top to bottom

**1. Decision Queue** — what only you can decide, oldest first, each
with a "needed by" date and whether the memo is ready. *This is the
section that matters most.* If something here is past its date, that's
the first thing to fix.

**2. Open Issues** — unresolved problems, each with an owner and an age.
Aging visibly is the point: nothing quietly disappears.

**3. Strategic Opportunities** — ideas parked *deliberately*, each with
the gate that would wake it up. Parked is not forgotten.

**4. Risk Snapshot** — the top five risks and which way they're
trending. Arrows up mean pay attention.

**5. Capital Register** — where the money-waterfall stands: compliance
floor, reserves, reinvestment, opportunity fund, distributions.

**6. Waiting-On List** — what's blocking *you* and who owes it.

**7. Personal Priorities** — **yours to write.** Claude never touches
this section.

**8. Reading List** — what feeds your thinking (and Summit Day 3).

**9. Travel** — trips with their gates and deadlines.

**10. Relationships** — the institutional relationships-of-record and
when each was last touched.

**11. Founder Notes** — **your private scratch space.** Claude reads it
only if you ask.

## The one habit

Once a week — Friday works — glance at sections 1 and 6: *what needs my
decision, and what am I waiting on?* Everything else can be a question
to Claude when you want it.

## The health rule

The dashboard shows its own `last-refreshed` date at the top. If it's
more than a week old, the system is failing silently — say so. An
unrefreshed dashboard is a red light on the Governance Scorecard,
visibly, by design.

## What to say to Claude

*"Show me my dashboard."* · *"What's past due in my queue?"* · *"Add to
my priorities: …"* · *"What am I waiting on, and for how long?"* ·
*"Refresh my dashboard."*
