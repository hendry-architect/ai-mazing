# The Annual Chairman's Declarations

> **The tradition, established:** every January 1, immediately following
> the Ascendencia Summit™, the Chairman issues and signs five
> institutional documents. Together they are the official historical
> record of the enterprise year — read at the next summit's opening,
> archived permanently (`office-of-the-chairman/declarations/YYYY/`),
> cross-referenced in PEGS-900's January edition, and excerpted in the
> Annual Stewardship Reading (the ten-minute ceremony). First signing:
> **January 1, 2027, Monte-Carlo.**

---

## 1. The Annual Declaration

```
ANNUAL DECLARATION — <YEAR>
Signed at <place>, January 1, <year>

THE YEAR COMPLETED (three sentences, honest)
THE DECADE LINE (one sentence to the 2036 steward)
THE YEAR AHEAD (three sentences of intent)
WHAT WE WILL NOT DO (one line — the discipline made ceremonial)

Signature — Dr. Hendry Pascual, Chairman
Witnessed by all present (signatures)
```

## 2. Enterprise Priorities

```
ENTERPRISE PRIORITIES — <YEAR>

The 3–5 priorities of the year, ranked, one line each — plain language
identical to the annual plan's objectives (the plan holds the detail;
this page holds the commitment). For each: the single number that will
prove it.

1. …
2. …

These priorities outrank everything not on this page.
Signature · date
```

## 3. Stewardship Commitment

```
STEWARDSHIP COMMITMENT — <YEAR>

What I hold in trust this year: the people, the patients, the family,
the canon, the name. (One paragraph, first person.)

What the next steward will inherit from this year that did not exist
before it. (One paragraph.)

The formation commitment: what I will teach, and to whom, this year.
(One or two lines.)

Signature · date
```

## 4. Capital Allocation Letter

```
CAPITAL ALLOCATION LETTER — <YEAR>
(conceptual directions; instruments per counsel — no tax/legal advice)

This year's waterfall intent: reserves (months target) · reinvestment
focus · opportunity fund posture · philanthropy commitment ·
distribution philosophy. One short paragraph per line item, with the
reasoning stated plainly enough to be argued with.

What changed from last year's letter, and why.

Signature · date
```

## 5. Letter to the Enterprise

```
LETTER TO THE ENTERPRISE — <YEAR>

The Chairman's open letter to every person of the Enterprise — employees,
providers, family, future stewards. Written in both languages (Dos
Idiomas, Una Voz). One page. The year's story, gratitude by name where
earned, and the year ahead as a shared endeavor — the one Declaration
written for everyone rather than for the record.

Signature · date
```

---

## Rules of the tradition

Signed January 1, in person, at the summit — never issued remotely or
late (a tradition kept loosely is lost) · each year's set archived
immutably; corrections are next year's honesty · the five documents are
short BY LAW of this template — one page each; the year's detail lives
in the plan, the book, and the records · PEGS-900's January edition
carries all five; the Stewardship Reading carries excerpts · the archive
of Declarations, read in sequence, IS the history of the Enterprise —
written one year at a time by the person who led it.
