# The Chairman's Briefs — four altitudes, four formats

> Compiled by the CGO from canon sources; never original data. The
> 15-minute contract governs the weekly; 45 minutes the monthly book.

## 1. Weekly Brief (Fridays — 1 page, 15 minutes)

```
WEEKLY BRIEF — week of <date>
1. THE WEEK IN THREE LINES     what actually mattered
2. DECISION QUEUE Δ            new items · items aging past need-by (red)
3. TRACKER PULSE               done/opened/overdue (names on overdue)
4. NEXT WEEK'S CALENDAR        governance events + prep due FROM you
5. ONE FLAG                    the single thing the CGO would worry about
```

## 2. Monthly Executive Brief (with the Monthly Review — feeds the Book)

```
MONTHLY BRIEF — <month>
1. SCOREBOARD SUMMARY          KPIs vs floors (from review §1)
2. DECISIONS MADE              D-IDs this month, one line each
3. RISK & COMPLIANCE Δ         register movements · 30-day lookahead
4. PILLAR NOTES                one paragraph per active pillar
5. SCORECARD                   governance G/Y/R deltas
6. THE MONTH'S LESSON          one, honestly, → knowledge base if durable
```

## 3. Quarterly Enterprise Review (the quarter close, chairman's cut)

The quarterly report (planning-system template) IS the instrument; the
chairman's cut adds one page on top:

```
QUARTERLY — <YYYY-QN>
1. THE QUARTER'S VERDICT       2 sentences, no hedging
2. WHAT COMPOUNDED             systems/assets that grew without attention
3. WHAT CONSUMED               where effort produced no compounding
4. NEXT QUARTER'S ONE BET      the single commitment that matters most
5. SCORECARD + MATURITY        gate progress (150.009)
```

## 4. Annual Stewardship Review (July, with the constitutional review)

The Founder's own annual account — the mirror of what he asks of every
executive:

```
ANNUAL STEWARDSHIP REVIEW — <year>
1. THE YEAR AGAINST THE MISSION      did the Enterprise sustain?
2. THE CONSTITUTION, LIVED           per-article honesty (from the review)
3. STEWARDSHIP LEDGER                what the next steward inherits that
                                     didn't exist last July
4. THE FOUNDER'S DELEGATION DELTA    what left his desk this year; what
                                     shouldn't still be on it
5. FAMILY & FORMATION                heir-path year notes (Trust-filed)
6. THE DECADE LINE                   one sentence to the 2036 steward
```

*Formats are canon; filled instances file to `records/` (annual
stewardship review → `06-Trust/family-records/` if it carries family
content).*
