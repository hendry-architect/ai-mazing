# 10 — Policies

Cross-enterprise policies: rules that bind every entity and person at once,
sitting between the Constitution (why) and SOPs (how).

**Belongs here** (expected first policies)

- Compliance & privacy policy (HIPAA/PHI handling, the Compliance Floor of
  PEGS-000 Art. VI §4.3)
- AI & automation use policy (implements the Automation Doctrine, Art. VI §5)
- Communication & social media policy (implements the Brand Firewall)
- Family employment policy (with `06-Trust`; earned-not-inherited)
- Vendor & data governance policy

**Rules of this folder**

- Policies are ratified by the Founder until the delegation matrix
  (`03-Executive-Leadership`) assigns policy custody.
- A policy that conflicts with PEGS-000 is void to the extent of the conflict
  (Art. VIII §1).

**Status:** Active — populated in Phase 4.
**Custodian:** Founder until delegated.
