# Enterprise Risk & Compliance

The Compliance Floor (PEGS-000 Art. VI §4.3) operationalized: know every
obligation, watch every named risk, rehearse every bad day.

**Library contents**

| Artifact | Use |
|---|---|
| [TEMPLATE-compliance-matrix.md](TEMPLATE-compliance-matrix.md) | Every regulatory obligation + the regulatory calendar (source of truth for *what*; the governance calendar schedules *when*) |
| [TEMPLATE-risk-register.md](TEMPLATE-risk-register.md) | Named risks, owners, mitigations — reviewed quarterly with planning |
| [TEMPLATE-insurance-register.md](TEMPLATE-insurance-register.md) | Every policy, limit, renewal, and gap |
| [TEMPLATE-business-continuity-plan.md](TEMPLATE-business-continuity-plan.md) | Keep-operating plan per entity |
| [SOP-incident-response.md](SOP-incident-response.md) | What happens in the first hour, day, and week of an incident |
| [TEMPLATE-cybersecurity-policy.md](TEMPLATE-cybersecurity-policy.md) | Security governance baseline |

**Operating rhythm**

- Monthly: compliance 30-day lookahead at the executive review §4 ⚙
- Quarterly: risk register review with the quarterly plan
- Annually: insurance renewal audit · BCP tabletop exercise · full matrix
  re-verification

**Standing rules:** there is no business case against the floor · every
obligation has one owner and one artifact proving compliance · incidents
surfaced early are never punished; incidents hidden always are.
