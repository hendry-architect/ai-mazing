# Amendment A1 — Verified Entity Structure — Close-out Reports

## 1. Executive Summary

Amendment A1 replaces every entity assumption in the PEGS canon with the
Founder's verified enterprise structure: **eight entities organized into
five permanent pillars** (Healthcare Services · Education · IP & Consumer
Health · Real Estate & Investments · Philanthropy & Legacy). PEGS-100 is
formally amended (v1.1.0, in review); the architecture suite (150.002/
.004/.005, PEGS-151, PEGS-152) is revised to match; a standardized Entity
Profile template is created and all eight profiles (PEGS-210.001–.008)
are drafted with verified data. Ratified governance philosophy is
untouched — only factual architecture changed. Major corrections: the
holding company, IPCo, and RE company **already exist** (Atemporal
Holdings LLC/WY, Holora Health LLC, Los Gonsos Royal Estate Corp);
Impactful Health Care Inc. is an **independent public charity**, not a
family vehicle; Pascual Foundation Inc. is formed with **IRS
determination pending**; Wellnex Academy LLC (education) joins the map.
