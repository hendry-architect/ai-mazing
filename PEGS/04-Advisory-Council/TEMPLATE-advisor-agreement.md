# Advisor Agreement — <Advisor name>

> Governance-side template. Counsel converts this into the executed legal
> instrument; the terms below are the Enterprise's required substance.

## 1. Confidentiality

- Everything shared in council settings is confidential indefinitely:
  strategy, financials, family matters, deliberations, and the fact that a
  matter was discussed.
- No PHI is ever shared with advisors — the clinical firewall (PEGS-000
  Art. VI §4.1) applies to this council absolutely.
- Materials remain the Enterprise's property; returned or destroyed on exit.

## 2. Conflict of interest

- **Standing disclosure:** advisor maintains a current list of boards,
  investments, clients, and family interests that could touch the
  Enterprise; refreshed annually and whenever circumstances change.
- **Per-topic recusal:** conflicted advisors state the conflict and sit out
  that agenda item; the recusal is minuted.
- **No trading on knowledge:** council information is never used for the
  advisor's or any third party's benefit.
- Undisclosed conflict discovered = automatic removal (charter §
  Appointment & removal).

## 3. Independence and candor

The Enterprise asks for the advisor's true view, especially in dissent.
Candor is the deliverable; agreement is not.

## 4. Term and exit

Per charter. Confidentiality survives exit indefinitely.
