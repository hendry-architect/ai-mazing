# SOP — Quarterly Advisory Council Meeting

Runs on the Meeting Kit (`11-Meetings/meeting-kit/`) with these parameters:

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Cadence | Quarterly, calendared a year ahead ⚙ |
| Duration | 3 hours (90 min business + 60 min one deep topic + 30 min open) |
| Chair / Secretary | Founder / Corporate Secretary function |
| Packet | T-7d (advisors are volunteers — give them more runway than staff) |

## Standing agenda

1. State of the Enterprise (Founder, 15 min — candid, packet §2 dashboard)
2. Prior advice: what we did with it (respect is shown by follow-through)
3. **The Question** — one major decision or strategic tension put to the
   council, framed in the packet with a Class 2 memo where applicable
4. Deep topic (rotates: capital, clinical, brand, technology, family)
5. Open counsel — anything an advisor believes the Founder isn't asking

## Rules

- Advice is attributed in the decision log (charter rule) — anonymity
  breeds safe blandness.
- No PHI, ever. Family matters only with the Founder's explicit framing.
- Follow-up report (T+48h ⚙) includes "advice → what we'll do → what we
  won't, and why."
