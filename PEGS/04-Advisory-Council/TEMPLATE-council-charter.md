# Strategic Advisory Council — Charter

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Purpose | Counsel to the Founder and executives — advice, never authority |
| Size | 3–7 advisors |
| Term | 2 years, renewable once by mutual agreement |
| Cadence | Quarterly meetings + annual retreat (see SOP + framework) |
| Compensation | <honorarium / equity-free by default / expenses> |

## Membership criteria

1. Domain mastery the Enterprise lacks internally (medicine, capital, media,
   law, technology — one seat should always challenge the Founder's
   strongest opinion).
2. Demonstrated integrity consistent with PEGS-000 Art. III.
3. No unresolved conflict of interest (see TEMPLATE-advisor-agreement.md).
4. Reads PEGS-000 before the first meeting — advises the Enterprise as it
   is, not as they'd rebuild it.

## Appointment & removal

- **Appointment:** Founder invites in writing after one working session;
  advisor signs the advisor agreement; seated at the next quarterly meeting.
- **Removal:** by the Founder at any time, or automatically on: breach of
  confidentiality, undisclosed conflict, or two consecutive unexcused
  absences. Removal is communicated privately and with dignity.

## Boundaries (the line that keeps counsel clean)

- No decision rights, no votes, no signature authority, no agency to bind
  the Enterprise.
- Not the Stewardship Council (PEGS-101) — advisors advise the living
  Founder; stewards succeed constitutional authority.
- Advice is recorded in the decision log; the decision is always the
  Founder's (or the accountable role's).

## Decision log rule

Every meeting's DECISIONS table (Meeting Kit) records: the question put to
the council, the range of advice (attributed), and what was decided —
including where the Founder decided against the council's advice, and why.
That record is how future stewards learn to weigh counsel.
