# Annual Retreat Framework (Council + Leadership)

The Q4 gathering where the Enterprise lifts its head: one to two days,
off-site, combining the advisory council's Q4 meeting with the leadership
annual planning session. Logistics run on the Travel & Retreat System
(`11-Meetings/retreats-travel/`); this framework fixes the substance.

## Arc (the order is the method)

| Block | Content | Output |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Look back | Annual index of decisions/actions (`records/<YYYY>/annual-index.md`); what we said vs. did | Honest year-in-review page |
| 2. Constitution hour | Annual constitutional review (SOP in `01-Constitution`) — with advisors present | Review record; amendments advanced or reaffirmation |
| 3. The big questions | 2–3 decade-scale questions framed by the Founder in the packet | Positions, not decisions — decisions get memos afterward |
| 4. Next year | Annual plan draft (planning-system TEMPLATE-annual-plan) pressure-tested by advisors | Annual plan v0.9 |
| 5. Family & legacy (closed session) | Founder + designated participants only | Notes to `06-Trust`, not the general record |

## Rules

- Retreat report within 72h (TEMPLATE in retreats-travel) — decisions,
  action items, owners, due dates, per the Meeting Kit chain.
- One evening is unstructured on purpose: the council's best advice
  historically arrives at dinner, not on slides.
- No retreat without the look-back block — vision unanchored from the
  record is theater.
