# 04 — Advisory Council

The Founder's external brain trust: advisors who counsel the Enterprise
without holding authority in it.

**Belongs here**

- Advisory Council charter: purpose, composition, terms, confidentiality
- Advisor onboarding packet (references PEGS-000 — every advisor reads the
  Constitution before advising against it)
- Advisory meeting cadence and standing agenda (minutes live in `11-Meetings`)

**Distinction that matters:** the Advisory Council *advises* and holds no
decision rights. It is not the Stewardship Council (`02-Governance/PEGS-101`),
which is the dormant successor to Class 1 constitutional authority. Keeping
counsel and control in separate bodies is deliberate.

**Status:** Reserved — chartered when the first advisors are seated.
**Custodian:** Founder.
